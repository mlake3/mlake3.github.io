Why it's My Favorite
I have always had a fascination with space. When Interstellar first came out in 2014, I absolutely loved it! After some time, I forgot how much I really loved the movie until it was re-released in theaters at the end of 2024. My roommate and I watched it together, and we both agreed it was absolutely amazing! No other movie has made me feel as inspired and moved.
