Figure Skating

Fun fact: I have been a competitive figure skater for almost my entire life! I began skating when I was just under 2 years old, and entered my first competition at age 3. By the time I was 9, I qualified for my first national championship. When I was 15, I qualified for Team USA and competed at my first international competition in the Netherlands.

Music

Music plays a huge role in my life. Not only do I skate to music, I also play it! I started playing piano when I was in kindgarten, and while it has been a rocky road, I still enjoy playing to this day. I am particularly interested in playing jazz piano, my favorite artists being Bill Evans, McCoy Tyner, and Oscar Peterson. I can also play cello and violin, though not nearly as well as I can play piano. Aside from jazz, I love listening to many genres, including classical, rock, indie, hip-hop, rap, R&B, blues, and alternative.

Other Sports

I am a huge basketball fan! I watch it almost every day, that is, when there are games actually going on. I also love to play golf.
